Join Us

Become a member

Members of the Association of Master Painters and Decorators are union contractors who receive the unique benefit of the Collective Bargaining Agreement. Membership includes benefits such as unlimited use of tools and defined working conditions consisting of scheduling, overtime rates and benefits.

Download the form to become a Member of the Association of Master Painters and Decorators here.

Associate Membership is available to suppliers, manufacturers and affiliates to receive the benefit of being in close and regular contact to the top contractors in the industry. A membership form is available here.

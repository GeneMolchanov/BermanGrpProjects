Message from the President

Dear Members,

The Holiday Season has arrived and the New York City Chapter of IFMA is excited to end the year on a high note, with several new events and opportunities to interact with other IFMA members and get engaged locally. As always, we encourage you to check our website for new updates, to register for upcoming events, find service providers and connect with fellow Chapter members.

Last month, the sustainability committee organized a panel discussion with the NYC Mayor’s office and Urban Green Council entitled, “Greener Greater Buildings,” which kicked off an upcoming webinar series the committee is working on.   The Chapter also hosted the Nominee Cocktail party for the Awards of Excellence nominees at the Knoll showroom.

Mark your calendars for our Holiday Party, which will be held on Tuesday, December 10, 2013 at the famous B. Smith’s restaurant.  Bring a toy or monetary donation to support IFMA Cares and Fraternite Notre Dame to be entered into our raffle.

I want to thank you all for your nominations for the Awards for Excellence dinner.  The Dinner and Awards committees are in full planning mode in preparation for our most anticipated event of the year on Thursday, January 24, 2014 at the Pierre Hotel.  We hope you will join us for a night of celebrating the accomplishments of the members you nominated this year.

It is an honor to serve the IFMA New York City Chapter. Over the past year, the enthusiasm and dedication of our membership has helped us maintain our status as the premier organization for Facility Managers and Professionals in the New York City area. We are immensely grateful for your support and guide of our misnsion and we look forward to next year with a new calendar of exciting programming and engaging networking events. We wish you all the best during the Holidays and in the upcoming New Year.

Gary Anzalone
President
